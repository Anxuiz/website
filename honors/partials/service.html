Service

In high school, I participated in the VEX Robotics Competition for 3 years. The competition is for high school and middle school students to develop their engineering ability by building small (18" cube) robots and competing them head-to-head.

To give back, I have been volunteering in the local robotics competitions each year. Usually I help out with inspection in the morning and refereeing throughout the day.
