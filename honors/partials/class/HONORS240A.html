Russian Crime Fiction

In this class I learned about Russian history and literature, and that it's possible to read 200 pages per week. We read eight books/short stories over the quarter. I re-affirmed that I do like reading literature and finally had a really compelling reason to finish Crime and Punishment.

Moreover, this class was important to me at the time because the reading provided inspiration for philisophical discussions about everyday things. I found the continual discussion about whether it is possible for there to be a "perfect crime" fascinating and insightful. The reasoning one goes through for analyzing this idea is analogous to that which applies for many philisophical issues.
