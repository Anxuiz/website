Evil Hangman

CSE 143 gives weekly programming assignments. Evil Hangman was the assignment from Week 4.

In normal Hangman, the first player picks a word that the second player has to guess. But what if the first player didn't pick a word at the beginning and tried to delay the choice for as long as possible? That's what we have here.
