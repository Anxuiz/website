Leadership, Democracy, and a More Thoughtful Public

Leadership, Democracy and a More Thoughtful Public stretched my ability to read, write, and think. I was not prepared for the sheer amount of writing in this class: 15 short, 1-2 page (single spaced!) response papers throughout the quarter culminating with a 10 page final paper. I had never done that much writing for a class before, so it was a shock the first few weeks. But I eventually learned how to manage the time commitment and produce good reflections.

After taking this class I gained the confidence to realize that page length does not really define how difficult a piece of writing will be. Now when I see "3-4 pages", I know that I need to spend most of my time researching rather than typing at the computer. A long (10-15 page) final paper is also no longer a shock.
