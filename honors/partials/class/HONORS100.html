Introduction to Honors Education

Honors 100 was a fun and necessary class for the first quarter at UW. It introduced me to many resources I would have never thought to look for on my own and gave me some tips for how to survive university life. Additionally, I got to meet an excellent group of students who I saw almost once a week.

I also enjoyed some of the assignments as it helped me understand better what options are available to me over the next four years. In particular, the 4 year plan was very helpful since I am considering a double major. I had a good suspicion it would be possible to complete in four years, and the four year plan confirmed that it's not just possible but also leaves room for other electives even after fulfilling the honors requirements!
